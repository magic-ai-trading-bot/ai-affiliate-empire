## COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

Use this checklist for EVERY piece of content:

### FTC Compliance
- [ ] Disclosure is clear and conspicuous
- [ ] Disclosure appears before affiliate links
- [ ] Disclosure uses plain, understandable language
- [ ] Disclosure cannot be missed by users
- [ ] Relationship between us and merchant is clear
- [ ] Terms like "ad", "affiliate", or "sponsored" are used

### Platform Compliance
- [ ] Platform-specific paid partnership tools used (if available)
- [ ] Hashtags include #ad or #affiliate
- [ ] Verbal disclosure in video content

- [ ] Description/caption contains disclosure

## WHAT NOT TO DO - Violations to Avoid

### ‚ùå NEVER Do These:

**1. Hidden Disclosures:**
- ‚ùå Placing disclosure at the bottom of long posts
- ‚ùå Using small, hard-to-read font
- ‚ùå Burying in "click to read more" sections
- ‚ùå Using ambiguous language like "partnerships"

**2. Misleading Practices:**
- ‚ùå Claiming products are "free" when you earn commission
- ‚ùå Fake reviews or testimonials
- ‚ùå Exaggerating product benefits
- ‚ùå Hiding the fact you haven't used the product

**3. Link Obfuscation:**
- ‚ùå Using link shorteners to hide affiliate parameters
- ‚ùå Redirects through multiple pages
- ‚ùå Removing affiliate disclosure from links

## LEGAL REQUIREMENTS SUMMARY

### FTC 16 CFR Part 255 Requirements:

**Material Connection:**
- Disclose any financial relationship
- Make disclosure before consumer acts
- Use clear, unambiguous language
- Ensure disclosure is unavoidable

**Clarity Standards:**
- "In close proximity" to affiliate claims
- "Unavoidable" by consumers
- "In the same language" as content
- "In plain English" (or applicable language)

### State-Specific Requirements:

**California (CCPA):**
- Additional transparency in data collection
- Disclosure of data sharing with affiliates
- Opt-out options for data sales

**Other States:**
- Follow most restrictive state requirements
- Assume disclosure needed everywhere
- Over-disclose rather than under-disclose

## RECOMMENDED PRACTICES

### Best Practices for Transparency:

1. **Be Early:** Disclose before the reader/viewer invests time
2. **Be Clear:** Use plain language, not legalese
3. **Be Consistent:** Use same disclosure style across all content
4. **Be Visible:** Make disclosure stand out, not blend in
5. **Be Honest:** Only recommend products you actually believe in

### Disclosure Timing:

**Video Content:**
- Verbal: First 3 seconds
- Visual: First 3 seconds on-screen
- Description: First 2 lines

**Written Content:**
- Before first affiliate link
- Above the fold (no scrolling needed)
- Repeated before major product sections
